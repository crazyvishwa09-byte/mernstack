TEXT-ALIGN

left: Aligns the text to the left edge of the containing element. This is the default alignment for left-to-right languages.

right: Aligns the text to the right edge of the containing element.

justify: Stretches the lines of text to fill the entire width of the containing element, creating a straight edge on both the left and right sides (similar to justified text in newspapers or magazines).
